Publications 发表

The Streaming Design Space and Evidence Boundaries: A Survey of Visual Token Compression for MLLMs

Anonymous Authors  ·  Under Review 匿名作者  ·  审稿中 In Submission 在投

A survey that reorganizes visual-token compression for multimodal large models around three structural dimensions (redundancy source, compression location, compression operation) and uses query timing and causality as the comparability boundary, accompanied by a Minimal Comparable Evaluation checklist for reporting latency, memory, and matched-budget baselines. 一篇关于多模态大模型视觉 token 压缩的综述：以"冗余来源—压缩位置—压缩操作"三个结构维度重新组织该领域,并以查询时序与因果性作为方法间可比性的边界,配套提出最小可比评估(MCE)清单,涵盖时延、显存与匹配预算基线等报告要求。

Selected Projects 精选项目

OCM: Streaming Visual Token Compression for MLLMs In Progress 进行中

A streaming visual token compression method for multimodal LLMs, evaluated on long-video benchmarks. A unified evaluation pipeline is being built around decord-based decoding, plug-in model adapters, and structured run-level metrics. 一种面向多模态大模型的流式视觉 token 压缩方法,目前正在长视频基准上评估。基于 decord 解码、模型插件化与结构化运行级指标,正在搭建统一的评测管线。

Polite Mask: Affect-Related Directions in Aligned LLMs

A mechanistic interpretability study of an affect-related direction in the residual stream of instruction-tuned LLMs, examining its causal role in compliance-sensitive behavior, cross-model transfer, and robustness across affect supervision sources. 对指令微调大语言模型残差流中"情感相关方向"的机制可解释性研究,考察其在依从相关行为中的因果作用、跨模型迁移性,以及在不同情感监督来源下的鲁棒性。
